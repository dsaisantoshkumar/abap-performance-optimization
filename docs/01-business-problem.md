Business Problem Definition

Organization Profile

The client operates a global manufacturing and distribution network with headquarters in North America and operations spanning 15 countries. The SAP S/4HANA system serves as the central ERP platform supporting finance, procurement, materials management, and sales operations. Daily active user count exceeds 2,000 employees across multiple time zones.

Performance Challenges

During the monthly financial close process, system performance degraded significantly between the 25th and 5th of each month. Users reported extended wait times when accessing critical transactions. Finance teams struggled to meet reporting deadlines due to slow system response. The procurement department experienced delays in purchase order processing that impacted supplier relationships.

Business Impact

The performance issues created cascading effects across the organization. Month end close procedures that previously completed in three business days now required five days. Financial reporting to senior management and board members faced delays. Procurement processing backlogs increased supplier payment cycle times. User productivity suffered as employees waited for transactions to complete.

Specific Symptoms

Financial posting transactions taking 8 to 12 seconds per document instead of expected 2 to 3 seconds. Standard financial reports timing out after 15 minutes without completing. Purchase order approval workflows experiencing delays of several minutes. Database CPU utilization reaching 95 percent during peak hours. Application server memory consumption trending upward over recent months.

Stakeholder Requirements

The finance leadership team established clear performance targets. Posting transactions must complete within 3 seconds. Standard reports must generate within 5 minutes. System must handle month end volume without degradation. Database resource utilization should remain below 70 percent during normal operations. Users must experience consistent response times regardless of processing volume.
